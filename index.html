A Trip to the Woods

Made by: Santiago Cordero
Source code in github

This is an adventure in which you will experience a trip to the woods gone wrong with your friends in a a choose-your-own-adventure format, where you will be presented with links to navigate to whatever choice you decide to make along the way (beware of the consequences).

Throughout this adventure, an unknown entity has left 3 codes marked with certain letters and numbers that, when assembled the correct way, will form a code that will reveal a deep secret hidden in a safe box that was just in your car for some reason. The only way to find these codes is by getting every good ending of the story.

The story begins with you and your friends Marcus, Josh and Sophie going on a road trip to Marcus' uncle's cabin located in the woods in your brand new 2020 Porsche 911 Cabriolet (absolute beauty of a car). While driving there, you pass by a mechanic shop and a gas station right next to each other, but you pay no mind to them as your gas tank is at more than half capacity (the trip took about a quarter your gas tank so far) and you guys bought snacks for the travel. As soon as you enter the forest with the cabin, you notice the check engine light turn on and the car starts making a faint unusual clanking noise whenever you switch gears. What do you do?

  • Keep going and look at the car when you get to the cabin
  • Call for a towtruck and postpone the trip
