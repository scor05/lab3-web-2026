A Horror? Adventure

This is an adventure in which you try to break all of the horror stereotypes of constantly picking the worst possible decision to do at every moment, for example: going to the basement when clearly there's something wrong, going back to the house after escaping, falling over when running away, and (astonishingly??) many more.

HOWEVER, there is a catch (of course there is a catch, otherwise this adventure would end in like 2 seconds), your character has been possessed by the evil supreme demon of hell
