Play During the Night

You decide to go ahead and ask your friends if they want to play with whatever both Marcus and his uncle decided to install on the cabin. To the left of the living room you found a table for table tennis, foosball and another table for beer pong, telling you that it was going to be a very occupied night.

Josh brought a bottle of tequila and a bottle of whiskey, daring you to a game of table tennis with two shots of each for the loser. There were already some beers in the house Marcus already left the previous day, which you guys used for the beer pong. All that you can remember is accepting Josh's deal and getting absolutely curb stomped, then everything else was a blur.

You then wake up, feeling absolutely wasted by the hangover you inflicted upon yourself. You look at the clock next to you, it marks only 8:03 AM, which surprises you a little given how much you believe you drank the night befre.

You also start to feel very hungry.
