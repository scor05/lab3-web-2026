A very peaceful breakfast

Heading over to the kitchen, you see all of your friends perfectly seated around a circular table, leaving the chair closest to you empty. You ask them: "morning guys, ¿yall ate anything yet?", but they don't respond at all.

Looking back at the table you see a very nice meal: some scrambled eggs with bacon and beans next to them. Thinking nothing of your friends' behaviour, you turn left towards the fridge to grab a refreshing beer (you guys forgot to actually bring real drinks, nice), but as you turn back you hear a loud CRACK as you see all of your friends' necks SNAP towards you, now looking at you with fully white eyes.

You freeze, thinking that this is all a hallucination, but after wiping your face multiple times and smacking yourself hoping to wake up, nothing changes. After what feels like an eternity, they all say in unison:

??? GET OUT GET OUT GET OUT
GET OUT GET OUT GET OUT
GET OUT GET OUT GET OUT

Still engulfed in fear, you say "of course I do, you guys are my childhood friends". However, upon thinking about it further, you don't really have any memories of them. It is all just blank.

Before you can even utter another sentence, they all jump at you and tackle you to the ground, after which, it's all dark
