Going to Bed

You tell your friends that you are too tired to play, but that you might want to the next day. You head over upstairs to the dorms and into the bed you made when you got to the cabin. After just a mere few minutes you find yourself knocked out cold

Wake However, in the middle of the night, you wake up randomly and the WHOLE HOUSE IS ON FIRE.

You panick and get up as fast as you can out of your bed and run towards the window and try to open it, but it doesn't budge. You yell for help and for your friends, no one comes but one black human figure you spot outside the cabin.

You blink and the figure is now standing behind you, it quickly grabs you by the shoulders and screams multiple times:

THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL
THEY ARE NOT REAL

After that, the entity teleports you to the main hallway and points to one of the walls before doing the gesture of opening a doorknob and suddenly phasing through the wall.

Suddenly, you wake up and realize all you saw just now was a nightmare, even if it seemed vivid enough to pass off as real life. The clock by your nightstand reads 8:03 AM, and you feel hungry.

What do you do?
  • Go to your car and book it out of there
  • Go down to eat breakfast and look for your friends
