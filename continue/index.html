Entering the forest

You continue into the forest, feeling a little uneasy about what could be happening to your car, but you continue nonetheless. Soon enough, you arrive at the cabin and start unpacking all of your luggage, one bag at a time.

As you walk into the cabin, you notice that it has a very unique decoration style, with most furniture being made out of wood and almost no interior lights but a single light bulb in the living room right in front of the door. It's nice, not the best you have seen but will definately make you comfortble for your stay. The cabin has stairs to your left hand side leading up to the dorms and a hallway that leads into the kitchen and a spa, which Marcus proudly claims to be entirely his idea to install.

After you finish getting everything out of the car and into the cabin, the day has almost fully passed, your watch marking 7:14 PM. You now have some spare time, what do you decide to do?

  • Play ping-pong with your friends and enjoy the night
  • Relax and go to bed
